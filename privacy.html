Privacy Policy for VocalDraft

VocalDraft respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding your information.

Information We Collect

- Voice recordings you make are processed locally on your device to generate transcriptions.
- Transcribed text is stored only on your device unless you choose to export or share it.
- We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or contact details.

How We Use Information

- Your voice recordings and drafts are used solely to provide the app’s core functionality: voice-to-text transcription and note taking.
- No data is uploaded to external servers by default.

Data Sharing

- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
- If you export or share drafts, you control where that data goes.

Security

- All data is stored locally on your device.
- Please use device-level security (such as a passcode or biometric authentication) to protect your drafts.

Children’s Privacy

VocalDraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
