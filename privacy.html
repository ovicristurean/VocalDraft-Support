  <h2>Information We Collect</h2>

  <p>

    - Voice recordings you make are processed locally on your device to generate transcriptions.<br>

    - Transcribed text is stored only on your device unless you choose to export or share it.<br>

    - We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or contact details.

  <h2>How We Use Information</h2>

  <p>

    - No data is uploaded to external servers by default.

  </p>


  <h2>Data Sharing</h2>

  <p>

    - We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.<br>

    - If you export or share drafts, you control where that data goes.

  </p>


  <h2>Security</h2>

    - All data is stored locally on your device.<br>

    - Please use device-level security (such as a passcode or biometric authentication) to protect your drafts.

  </p>
